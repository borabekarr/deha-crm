## Quick context

Deha is the **"Smartest CRM"** — bilingual (Turkish UI strings + English product chrome), mobile-first, with a single emerald (`#10B981`) accent over translucent white "liquid glass" cards. Montserrat Black does the heavy lifting; numbers are the heroes. No emoji, ever — iconography is Material Icons + Material Symbols Outlined.

When in doubt, open `ui_kits/crm-mobile/index.html` — it is the canonical reference and reuses every token and component.
